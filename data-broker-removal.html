Why data broker removal matters

  • Data brokers distribute addresses, relatives, and phone numbers at scale.
  • Listings can be used for identity theft, harassment, and targeted scams.
  • Broker networks share data, which multiplies exposure across many sites.

How removal works

Discovery

Locate listings across people‑search sites, broker databases, and public records.

Verified opt‑outs

Submit requests using each broker’s specific identity‑verification requirements.

Monitoring

Track re‑listings and re‑submit removals when data reappears.
