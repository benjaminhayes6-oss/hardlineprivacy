Trust & Security

A high‑level overview of how Hardline Privacy handles data, opt‑outs, and incident response.

Leadership accountability

Hardline Privacy is led by Grey, a U.S. Military Veteran and former law‑enforcement professional. Security reviews, opt‑out workflows, and monitoring protocols are owned at the founder level.

Data handling overview

Minimum data collection

We collect only what is necessary to locate and remove listings or respond to inquiries.

Secure storage

Client data is handled with least‑privilege access and retained only as needed for service delivery.

No resale

We do not sell personal information or share it with advertisers.

Opt‑out mechanics

Verified submissions

Opt‑out requests are submitted to third‑party sites using their required verification steps.

Follow‑ups & monitoring

We track status and re‑submit when data reappears or a broker re‑lists information.

No guarantees

Removal outcomes depend on third‑party sites; timelines and results vary by source.

Incident response posture

Monitoring

We monitor for suspicious activity and investigate anomalies promptly.

Notification

If an incident impacts client data, we will notify affected parties as required by law.

Contact

Security inquiries can be sent to support@hardlineprivacy.com.
