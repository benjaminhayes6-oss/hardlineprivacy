Pricing FAQ

Do plans include protection if brokers relist data later?

Continuous Monitoring and Household Protection include relist detection and re-removal requests. Core Removal focuses on initial takedown operations.

Is there a long-term contract?

Core Removal is one-time, Continuous Monitoring is month-to-month, and Household Protection is annual. No multi-year contract is required.

Can a plan be upgraded later?

Yes. Many clients start with a one-time cleanup, then move into monitoring once initial removals are verified.
