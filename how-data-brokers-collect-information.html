How Data Brokers Collect Information

Broker datasets are assembled through lawful but opaque collection channels that most households never directly see.

Investigative Breakdown

Collection sources typically include public records, commercial lead vendors, loyalty programs, ad-tech identifiers, and licensed partner feeds. Collected fields are merged to expand profile depth and confidence.

Exposure Implications

The breadth of intake means one household can be visible across dozens of directories even without creating accounts on those sites.

Exposure mapping should start with a structured scan, followed by targeted removals and periodic verification. Hardline documents plan details on Pricing.
