Why Data Brokers Are Legal

Most U.S. data-broker activity is legal because the source data is purchased, licensed, or collected from records that are lawfully accessible.

Investigative Breakdown

Compliance frameworks vary by state, and many requirements emphasize notice and opt-out mechanisms rather than prior consent. As a result, legality does not equal low exposure risk.

Exposure Implications

Effective response focuses on exercising available opt-out rights and verifying enforcement across participating brokers.
