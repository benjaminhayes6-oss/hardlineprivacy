What we do to protect your privacy

We handle the time‑consuming work of submitting opt‑out requests to 700+ data broker sites, people‑search sites, and public records aggregators.

Our services

People‑Search Removal

We identify and submit opt‑out requests to high‑risk people‑search sites that publish your name, address, phone number, and relatives.

  • Major people‑search sites and broker networks
  • Verified opt‑out submissions
  • Follow‑up until removal is confirmed

Google Result Removal

We file removal requests with Google to suppress personal information appearing in search results, including outdated or sensitive data.

  • Google personal‑info removal requests
  • Search result suppression
  • Outdated content flagging

Street View & Maps

If your home is visible on Google Street View, we submit blur requests to reduce your physical‑location exposure.

  • Google Street View blur requests
  • Property imagery suppression
  • Applicable where imagery is available

Ongoing Monitoring

Data brokers frequently re‑list removed information. Our monitoring service catches re‑listings and handles re‑removal automatically.

  • Quarterly re‑scans of major brokers
  • Automatic re‑removal submissions
  • Monthly status alerts

Enhanced Protection

For high‑risk individuals — law enforcement, military, first responders, public‑facing professionals — we provide expanded coverage and priority handling.

  • 700+ broker site coverage
  • Priority handling and verification
  • Enhanced documentation packet

Documentation & Proof

We provide documentation of removals completed, including confirmation screenshots and status reports where feasible.

  • PDF proof packets
  • Removal confirmation records
  • Status reporting
