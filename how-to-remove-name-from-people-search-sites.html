Operational guide to removal workflows, verification steps, and sustained suppression controls.

How the Removal Process Works

Map profile variants by name, city, and age range; submit opt-outs across each platform; then verify suppression on direct URLs and search indexes. Track response windows and unresolved records.

Why Continuous Monitoring Is Necessary

People-search platforms share data pipelines, so one unresolved source can repopulate multiple sites. Recurring checks are required to sustain removals.
