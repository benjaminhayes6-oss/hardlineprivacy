Understanding the problem

How data brokers work

Brokers aggregate records from public and commercial sources, then republish profile data at scale.

What is commonly exposed

Name, address history, phone numbers, relative associations, and household-level identifiers.

Why listings return

Re-ingestion cycles and partner feeds can republish records after previous removals.

Practical sequence

1. Scan

Start with a baseline. Use the free exposure scan to identify high-level public visibility.

2. Remove

Submit verified opt-outs across priority brokers and record each request path.

3. Monitor

Re-check and re-submit as listings reappear through broker refresh cycles.

4. Harden

Limit future data leakage through account settings and registration hygiene.
