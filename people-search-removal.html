What people-search sites publish

  • Home addresses, phone numbers, and age ranges.
  • Relatives, past roommates, and associated addresses.
  • Work history, social profiles, and public record links.

Risks of public listings

Targeting and harassment

Bad actors use listings to locate and contact households.

Identity misuse

Exposed details can be used for impersonation and account takeovers.

Re‑distribution

People‑search sites often share data with brokers and affiliates.

Removal workflow

Locate listings

Search across variations of names, addresses, and aliases.

Submit opt‑outs

Follow each site’s verification steps and required documentation.

Monitor for re‑listing

Many sites republish data when they refresh sources.

Hardline Privacy approach
