Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers about removals, timelines, and how Hardline Privacy works.

What does Hardline Privacy do?

We locate publicly available personal information listed on data broker and people-search sites and submit removal requests where available.

Are removals permanent?

No service can guarantee permanent removal. We reduce exposure and monitor for re-listings.

How long do removals take?

Most removals begin within 7–21 days depending on verification requirements.

Who is the service for?

Families, professionals, veterans, and households protecting children and aging parents.

Do you access private databases?

No. Only publicly accessible information and official opt-out channels are used.

Common questions

Can I do this myself?
Yes, but most people stop after a few sites. Many require verification and repeated follow-ups.
How long does it take?
Initial removals typically complete in 7–21 days depending on the site.
Will my data come back?
Often yes — which is why monitoring and re-removal matter.
