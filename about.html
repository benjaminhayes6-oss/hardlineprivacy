Mission

Our mission is to give people back control over their personal data.
Hardline Privacy finds and suppresses the listings that put families at risk—people‑search pages, data‑broker dossiers, and public‑record aggregators—and keeps working as new exposures appear. We combine focused search coverage, verified opt‑outs, and clear status reporting so anyone can reduce their online footprint without becoming a privacy expert. Our veteran‑founded team prioritizes security, accountability, and practical results.

Founder-led accountability

Hardline Privacy is led by Grey, a U.S. Military Veteran and former law‑enforcement professional. The team applies operational security discipline to every removal request, verification step, and monitoring cycle.
