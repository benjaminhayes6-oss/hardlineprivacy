How to Lock Down Personal Information Online

Control-focused guidance for reducing personal-data exposure across households and public records ecosystems.

Core Protection Controls

Lock-down work prioritizes high-impact identifiers first: residence data, direct phone numbers, and household links. It then extends to secondary data trails across directories and public-record aggregators.

Implementation Workflow

Maintain a documented playbook with removal status tracking, verification checkpoints, and escalation actions for records that repeatedly reappear.
